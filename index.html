How To Track Ambulance

It will categorize the no. of the ambulance to be present at a specific location on the basis of Demographic analysis and Route Optimization Algorithm. It will also check the ambulance’s condition, track the ambulances through GPS & demographics analysis. With the analysed data, the ambulances can be positioned in such a way that they cover a location suitably and efficiently. The placing of ambulances will be according to Resource Allocation Algorithm, which would effectively save both time and money.
For example, if all the ambulances in a vicinity are busy then an ambulance from a less prone area (as assessed by demographics) will arrive at the required location as quickly as possible.

How To Find Patient

for determining the patient's location, we'll use an algorithm which locates the nearest spot from the patient where an ambulance can reach. This will help both the patient and the ambulance to speed up the treatment. If the patient has a device without GPS, then the location will be identified on the basis of nearest network tower.

Non-Medical Problem

To recognize the patient’s condition, our automated system will use audio analysis to classify the symptoms from the patient's call and match them to understand which disease the patient might have and what kind of ambulance is to be sent for help. We will develop an Artificial Intelligence algorithm which will monitor the severity of the patient's disease on the basis of patient's call. For example, if a person says that the patient is having a pale body and crumbled fingers then he/she would be more severe than a person who is suffering from a body pain or a simple headache.
